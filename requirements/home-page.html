2.1.4. Instructor Home Page

When an instructor successfully logs into Test Tool, the landing screen in Figure 4 will appear.

Figure 4: Instructor home screen.


The home screen displays three buttons that lead to three major menus - the test directory, student directory, and question bank. Clicking on any one of the buttons will take the instructor to the respective menu.
