2.4.5. Creating an Essay Question

To create an essay question, the instructor opens the question creator dialog, and then changes the question type to Essay. The instructor will see Figure 11.

Figure 11: Essay view.


For essay questions, the instructor only creates a question. The instructor grades these types of questions by hand, so no additional information is necessary.
